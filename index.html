About Me



My dream is to create beautiful things and get enough sleep. In pursuit of my dream, I am tirelessly working towards a career in Web Development through Eleven Fifty Academy, with hopes to also cultivate my skills in UX/UI Design.

I have a BFA in Fine Arts and I'm also training to become a licensed financial advisor. One day the concept of the 'starving artist' be a thing of the past. I plan to be part of that reform in the world of tech by providing financial literacy via beautiful design.
